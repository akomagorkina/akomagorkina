[**Driven**](https://aleksandra.tech/apps/driven/) is a driving journal that automatically logs your trips, maps them, and organises them by vehicle — building a personal driving history of distance, hours behind the wheel, favourite destinations, and spending. A "Driving Passport" sums up your year, and CSV export covers taxes and expenses.

Built with SwiftUI. *Your trips stay on your device — no accounts, no ads, no tracking.* [App&nbsp;Store&nbsp;→](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/driven-driving-hours-tracker/id6784777044)
